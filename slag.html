Nike Dunk Low Skate Like A Girl

The Skate Like Girl Dunk is a women's-exclusive colorway of the classic sneaker that is inspired by the Skate Like Girl movement. The movement is dedicated to empowering young women and trans people to skate and to promote equality in skateboarding. The "Skate Like Girl" Dunk features a White leather upper with Barely Rose and University Red suede overlays. The Swoosh is also suede and is in Barely Rose. The tongue and collar are made of plush corduroy in Barely Rose. The laces are also in Barely Rose. The heel tab features a Skate Like Girl graphic. The outsole is in Gum. Release Date: October 18, 2022

Size: 7W
