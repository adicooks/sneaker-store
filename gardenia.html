Nike Dunk Low Fly Streetwear Gardenia

The Women’s Nike Dunk Low “Needlework” is a women’s-exclusive colorway of the classic sneaker that is inspired by women’s fashion. An endlessly versatile shoe, the “Needlework” features a Sail leather base with contrasting Photon-Dust-colored leather overlays. A Photon Dust leather Swoosh with textured detailing appears on the sides. Braided rope laces give the shoe a casual yet elegant look. “Nike” branding is debossed in script lettering on the heel. Release date: April 12, 2023

Size: 7W
